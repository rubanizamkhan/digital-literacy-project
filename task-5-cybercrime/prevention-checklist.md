### **Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist**



##### **General Safety Tips**

\- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts  

\- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for extra security  

\- Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links  

\- Always check the website URL before entering personal details  

\- Do not share sensitive information on untrusted platforms  

\- Keep your devices, apps, and antivirus software updated  



##### **Financial / UPI Safety Tips**

\- Never share your OTP, PIN, or banking details with anyone  

\- Always verify payment requests before accepting them  

\- Use only official and trusted payment applications  

\- Regularly monitor your bank account and transaction history  



##### **Reporting Cybercrime**

\- Report cybercrime incidents at: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in  

\- Contact the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (available 24x7)
